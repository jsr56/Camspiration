About Us

Pick-me-up : Your trusty cocktail of motivation

By Camspiration: Henna Noor, Sakinah Merali, and Jai Rane

We are creating a motivational self-care page for Cambridge Students. We will be curating different forms of media (comfort food recipes, songs, inspirational quotes, recommended reads etc) to provide a one-stop hub for students seeking comfort after a rough day. Each TLC package will be personalized to address different situations every Cambridge student has experienced at least once for eg. 'My supervision went badly', 'I feel like I don't belong here', 'I'm homesick' etc We also want to add an option for site visitors to add their own quotes and media to the website so everything can ultimately by crowd-sourced.
